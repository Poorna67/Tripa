About Us

TriPA Lead Gen Pros was founded on experience, collaboration, and a shared vision for growth.

In 2020, Teja Ande, Anand Babu, and Poorna Chandra became roommates after joining the same organization. While working together, each of us specialized in different growth-focused segments—lead generation, market research, and email marketing—gaining hands-on experience across diverse industries and global markets. Over the next five years, we collectively helped companies generate qualified leads, strengthen sales pipelines, and drive measurable revenue growth.

As our experience deepened, we recognized a common challenge faced by many businesses: access to high-quality, sales-ready leads backed by accurate data and effective outreach. What began as conversations between teammates soon evolved into a clear plan—build a company that delivers growth solutions that actually work.

That vision led to the creation of TriPA Lead Gen Pros — Built by Three, Focused on Growth.

Today, we support B2B and B2C businesses across all regions, partnering with startups and established companies to design scalable lead generation systems. We work across multiple industries, delivering both custom-built databases tailored to client requirements and proprietary, ready-to-use datasets developed through our in-house research processes.

To further strengthen outreach performance, we have also developed our own email marketing infrastructure, enabling secure, compliant, and high-volume campaign execution with better control over deliverability and results.

At TriPA, we don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Every strategy we design is rooted in data, aligned with business goals, and focused on long-term growth. Our journey began as a shared workspace—and has grown into a trusted growth partner for businesses worldwide.
